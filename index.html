FAQ

How does pricing work?

I offer both package deals and per-video pricing depending on your needs. Packages are great for ongoing content (weekly videos, monthly campaigns), while per-video pricing works well for one-off projects. Contact me for a custom quote based on your content type, length, and complexity.

What's the typical turnaround time?

For short-form content (TikTok/Reels), expect 2-3 business days. YouTube videos typically take 5-7 business days depending on length and complexity. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee. I'll always communicate realistic timelines upfront.

How many revisions are included?

All projects include 2 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied. Additional revisions can be added if needed. My goal is to get it right, so I'm flexible and collaborative throughout the process.

How do we share content and files?

I work with Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, or Frame.io—whatever you're most comfortable with. You'll share raw footage and assets, and I'll deliver final videos in your preferred format and resolution. All files are handled securely and professionally.

Do you work with new creators or only established channels?

I work with creators at all stages! Whether you're just starting out or have millions of followers, I tailor my approach to your current needs and growth goals. Everyone starts somewhere, and I love being part of that journey.
